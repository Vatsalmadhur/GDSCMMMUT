A bit About us!

Google Developer Student Clubs are university based community groups for students interested in Google developer technologies. Students from all undergraduate or graduate programs with an interest in growing as a developer are welcome. By joining us, students grow their knowledge in a peer-to-peer learning environment and build solutions for local businesses and their community.

What do we do?

We organise sessions, seminars, workshops and other fun activities to help students learn,grow and develope new skills!
